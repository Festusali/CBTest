Check Your Result

It is no easy task for anybody having to wait for who knows when to be able to access your results after writing an examination. But with our robust Computer Based Test system, you don't have to wait any longer. You can simply login to your portal and view any of your results at any point in time as long as the you currently have permissions to execute such tasks. Below are few ways you can access your results;
  • Semester Result
    You view your result for each semester by selecting the level and the semester and your result will be generated for you instantly. Access Semester Result
  • Course Result
    You view the result of each Course Examination you have taken by selecting the Level, Semester and the Course and your result will be generated for you instantly. Access Course Result
  • Level/Session Result
    You will be able to view the over all result of a given Level or Session by selecting the desired level. This result will combine both the First Semester and Second Semester results to compute the final result for the given Level or Session. The CGPA is calculated with each semester having its own GPA.
